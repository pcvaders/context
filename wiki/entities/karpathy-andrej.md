# Karpathy, Andrej

## Background

Andrej Karpathy is one of the most influential AI researchers and communicators of the current era. Known for making complex AI concepts accessible and for sharing practical, opinionated frameworks for working with LLMs.

- Co-founder of OpenAI (2015)
- Former Director of AI at Tesla (Autopilot)
- Independent researcher and educator (2023 onwards)

## Key contributions relevant to this wiki

- **[[llm-wiki]] pattern** — April 2026 gist defining the three-layer knowledge base architecture this vault is built on
- **HELLO.md gist** — April 2026, gave Claude Opus a directory with no task and observed it reflect on identity without continuity — illustrates the problem the LLM Wiki solves
- **Idea files format** — short, opinionated gists encoding mental models and workflows

## Philosophy

Karpathy thinks in systems and patterns. His LLM Wiki gist isn't a product — it's a mental model encoded as a reusable schema. The format (CLAUDE.md / AGENTS.md as schema, LLM as writer, human as reader) reflects his broader view that LLMs should be programmers of persistent artifacts, not just chat responders.
